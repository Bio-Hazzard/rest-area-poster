REDUCING THE
RISING DEATH TOLL

It is assumed that one of the biggest killers
on the road is drink driving

Did you know that the actual killer is driver fatigue?

"In 2012 more people in NSW died in fatigue-related crashes than drink driving crashes."

The issue

Although rest areas have been introduced they're poorly executed and provide inadequate services.
Many drivers simply don't feel comfortable stopping

Who this matters to?

Fatigue effects all drivers and with 17 633 493 motor vehicles on the road in 2014 its vital we fix this issue
